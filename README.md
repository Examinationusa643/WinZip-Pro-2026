## 🔧 Troubleshooting

*   **App fails to open:** Ensure your Windows operating system is up to date. Restart your computer and try again.
*   **Encryption password lost:** You cannot recover a lost password for an encrypted archive. Keep your passwords in a secure location.
*   **Slow performance:** Close unnecessary programs to free up RAM. Large files require more time to compress or verify.
*   **Permission errors:** Run the application as an administrator if the software cannot access folders on your system drive.
*   **Cloud upload failed:** Verify your internet connection and check that your cloud service account is active.
